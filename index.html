Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a taxi with Saarthi Taxi Hub?

You can easily book by filling out the form on our homepage, clicking the WhatsApp button, or calling us directly at +91 8923425020. We recommend booking in advance during festive seasons.

Are there any hidden charges like tolls or taxes?

Our base per km rate does not include tolls, state taxes, or parking fees. These are usually charged extra as per actuals based on the route taken. Driver allowance is applicable for night journeys and long outstation trips.

Do you provide cabs for Delhi Airport drop/pickup?

Yes, we provide 24x7 airport transfer services from Mathura and Vrindavan to Delhi IGI Airport and Agra Airport. We ensure timely pickups so you never miss your flight.

Can I customize my Braj Darshan tour?

Absolutely! We specialize in customized pilgrimage tours. Tell us which temples (Banke Bihari, Prem Mandir, etc.) or places you want to visit, and we will plan the optimal route and vehicle for your group.
